## Logo idea

The VFA mark is built as a golden-ratio institutional symbol.

- V: protective institutional frame
- F: intelligence pillar and knowledge structure
- A: ascending bridge and agency
- Orbit: global digital trust network
- Center seal: governance, verification, command core

## Golden ratio construction

The mark uses three proportional layers:

```text
Outer radius = 1.000
Inner radius = 1 / 1.618
Small radius = 1 / 1.618 / 1.618
```

The clear space is based on the central seal radius.

- Use PNG for app stores, social previews, and raster fallbacks.

- Do not distort the mark.

- For sub-brands, use VFA as endorsement rather than competing with IAI.ONE.
